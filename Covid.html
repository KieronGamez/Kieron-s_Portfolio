What is Covid-19

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. Most people who fall sick with COVID-19 will experience mild to moderate symptoms and recover without special treatment. However, some will become seriously ill and require medical attention.

What happens when you body is infected with Covid-19

When covid enters the body, usually through inhaling the covid particles or having physical contact with someone or something that had covid on it, will travel from the nose and mouth into multiple organs, however, it can do the most damage in the lungs. There, the covid cells will target the epithelial cells, which line your organs. The Covid cells will then inject their genetic DNA into the cell, infecting it and causing the cell to be confused, but will unknowingly be making more and more covid cells. Eventually, the cell will self-destruct and release the covid cells into the rest of the epithelial cells and infect the billions of cells in the lungs.

What are the symptoms of Covid-19?

Fever or chills, cough, shortness of breath or difficulty breathing,fatigue, muscle or body aches, headache, new loss of taste or smell, sore throat, congestion or runny nose, nausea or vomiting, diarrhea

How has Covid-19 affected the world?

Covid has sent the world into a panic, it has caused the closure of countries, the stop of vital social contact people could have, close down businesses, killed people and ruined families. People have protested in the streets, looting was rampant for a while and a general distrust of everyone formed. However, in recent year, we have managed to slowly fend off Covid-19 and slowly bring about a new normal.

What can we do to prevent the spread

Social distancing, wash your hands, wear a mask, stay indoors. While these may seem simple, they can do alot to protect yourself and your loved ones.
